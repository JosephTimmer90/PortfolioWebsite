About Me

I am studying computer science to become a web developer! Currently I am a delivery driver. Although, I enjoy my work and the people I work with I feel web development would be a much more rewarding career. My love of coding makes it easy to motivate myself to study after a long day of delivering packages.

My interests include:

  • Excercising.
  • Studying computer science.
  • Listening to music.
  • Grilling.
  • and, keeping my room clean.

I have been studying web development for several months. My proudest accomplishment to date is a game called Tic-Tac-Joe. It is a tic-tac-toe game I made myself using javaScript.

My career began in engineering. I did well for many years. I began to have mental health issues which made it difficult for me to maintain employment. I have been through rough times, but I have gotten the help I need and am now doing better than ever! With the time I have had to reflect on my life I know that web development is the career for me.
